About Me

I am a rising fourth-year student majoring in Cognitive Science with a specialization in Computing. My research interests center on how mental encoding states influence the cognitive mechanisms underlying memory formation, event segmentation, and recall. I am particularly interested in the intersection of music and cognition. In my spare time I enjoy playing drums and guitar, kicking a soccer ball around, and traveling to new places whenever I get the chance.
